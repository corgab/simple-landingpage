Privacy Policy

This privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) describes how the website owner (“we” or “the site”) collects, uses, and protects users' personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable laws.

1. Types of Data Collected

Through the form on our site, managed by the external platform Tally.so, we collect the following personal data:

  • First name and last name
  • Email address
  • Other data that the user voluntarily decides to share

2. Purpose of Data Processing

The collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Evaluating interest in our project and improving our services.
  • Sending occasional communications (e.g., updates or newsletters).

3. Data Processing Methods

The data is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently. The processing is done through digital tools provided by Tally.so. For more details on the processing done by Tally.so, please refer to their Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing

The collected data will not be sold or shared with third parties, except with essential service providers (e.g., Tally.so), who process the data on our behalf and according to our instructions.

5. Users' Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of data.
  • Object to the processing of data or limit its use.
  • File a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority (in Italy: the Garante for the Protection of Personal Data).

To exercise these rights, you can contact us via email at: support@craftedhub.it.

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be published on this page.
